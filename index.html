Meet Thalia

For three years, I've been perfecting the art of nail design in Jersey City, building a reputation for precision and creativity that keeps clients coming back.

My signature "iced" aesthetic combines technical excellence with artistic vision, transforming each nail into a miniature masterpiece. Every design tells a story, and I'm here to help you tell yours.

From the bustling streets of Jersey City to the fashion-forward clients who trust me with their vision, I bring the same dedication to every appointment: meticulous attention to detail and a passion for perfection.
